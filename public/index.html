Welcome to Wish Well

Crowdfunding community projects with your well wishes.

Community Projects

Support a project that needs to raise funds such as supplies for painting, fixing a raised sidewalk, or flood damage recovery, Wish Well can help collect donations from the community.

Donate

Visit a project's well and toss a donation for good will.

Well Wishes

Leave a goodwill message, or a wish for the project.

Each time you donate, you'll create a ripple effect that passes on your good wishes to the communities around you.
